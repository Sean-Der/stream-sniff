streamsniff

Streamsniff analyzes your streaming video so you can get the best quality possible. It inspects what you send and explains, in plain language, how it could be better.

  • See whether your video looks overly compressed.
  • Check the resolution and H.264 profile you are sending.
  • Get practical recommendations and the trade-offs behind them.

Use the Service, Server, and Stream key shown here when configuring OBS or FFmpeg.
