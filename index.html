Stream Sniff

Stream Sniff analyzes your streaming video and audio so you can get the best quality possible. It inspects what you send and explains, in plain language, how it could be better.

  • See whether your video looks overly compressed.
  • Check the resolution and H.264 profile you are sending.
  • Check audio levels, clipping, and silence detection.
  • Get practical recommendations and the trade-offs behind them.
